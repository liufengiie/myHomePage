I received my bachelor degree of computer science in 2003 from Shandong University and received my Ph.D degree of information security in 2009, under supervision of Professor Chuankun Wu, from Institute of Software (IOS), CAS. I'm currently a professor and Ph.D supervisor of Institute of Information Engineering (IIE), CAS. I'm also serves as a professor of School of Cybersecurity, Univeristy of Chinese Academy of Sciences.
My current research interests include: AI for cybersecurity, cryptography, cyber attack and defense. I am looking for opportunities to cooperate with researchers within my research interests all over the world.
